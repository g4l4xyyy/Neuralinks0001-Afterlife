Could The Neuralinks show what happens after Death?
Neuralinks. What are they? Are they safe? Can they really show after death
experiences? Neuralinks are new implants that can be added to your brain and read your mind.
These little chips will be out in the year 2022 as Elon Musk, Neuralink CEO, says. A lot of people
are not comfortable of adding an implant to the human brains. The immune system may identify
the neuralink as an “enemy” and try to destroy it. It may lead to bigger problems in the human
brain and body. Others find this lack of privacy. Another opinion is that either Elon Musk will
control us all or we could easily get hacked. Neuralinks can only read thoughts and memories
and cannot edit, add, or even delete. So, the best thing a hacker can possibly do is to learn some
things that you would not be comfortable sharing. You can charge them wirelessly. This means,
what if, we had a summary of the neuralink when someone has passed away. Could we connect
to the brain and figure out what is going on after we die? One of the following scenarios can be
true.
Scenario A: In this scenario, we get image of what the brain thinks during someone is
dead.
Scenario B: In this scenario, the brain shows a no signal error because it gets no energy.
Scenario C: In this scenario, the brain shows image. It is just an empty place; it could be
described as “The Void” of the Backrooms. Black is a color, so it is not empty.
If scenario A or C are correct, then humanity has gone a step further. We might even get
a higher level on the Kardashev Scale.
If scenario B is correct, then nothing has happened, and humanity needs to do more
research.
